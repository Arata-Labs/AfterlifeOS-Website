#NeverDie

More Than Just An OS

AfterlifeOS wasn't born in a boardroom. It was forged in late-night chats, shared debugging sessions, and a shared belief that our devices deserve better.

Born from Family

In a world of fleeting updates and planned obsolescence, we stood together. What started as a small group of enthusiasts tweaking kernel parameters evolved into a brotherhood.

We don't just build ROMs; we build relationships. Every line of code is committed with trust, knowing that someone on the other side of the screen relies on it for their daily life. That trust is our most valuable asset.

Our Core Values

Brotherhood

We leave no one behind. Whether you're a dev or a user, once you're here, you're family.

Trust

Transparency is key. Open source, open communication, and zero hidden agendas.

Longevity

The #NeverDie philosophy. We strive to keep devices alive and performing long after their EOL.

The #NeverDie Spirit

It's not just a slogan. It's a promise. A promise that as long as there is code to be written and a community to support it, the Afterlife will continue.
